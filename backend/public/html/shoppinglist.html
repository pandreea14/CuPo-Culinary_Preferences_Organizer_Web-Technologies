Shopping list - help

This page is specifically meant to help you keep track of your shopping lists. Simply add new items to it, either by manually introducing them, or by seaching for them in the main menu. You can also navigate your favorites and add them from there! When you are done with this shopping list, or you simply want another one, feel free to click on the "Create new shopping list" button.
Happy shopping!
